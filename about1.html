About BMI Calculator



Welcome to our BMI calculator, the easy way to calculate your body mass index. Our tool is simple to use, just enter your weight and height and we will do the rest.

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that is commonly used by healthcare professionals to determine whether a person is underweight, overweight or at a healthy weight. BMI provides a simple and effective way to evaluate weight status and is widely used in adults and children aged 2 and older.

Our calculator uses the following formula to calculate BMI: BMI = weight / (height^2). Once you have entered your details, we will display your BMI score along with information on what it means and any potential health risks associated with your weight status.

We hope this tool helps you track your weight and maintain a healthy lifestyle. If you have any questions or concerns please don't hesitate to reach out to us via our contact form.
